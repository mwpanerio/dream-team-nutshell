Frequently asked questions

How can I import my contacts into Nutshell?

Getting your people into Nutshell from any source is simple—just put your contacts into a CSV file, select the appropriate Nutshell fields, and import away! We have a helpful video that walks through the process, or you can reach out to our support team for assistance.

Is there a limit to the number of contacts that I can add to Nutshell?

Nope! Nutshell comes standard with unlimited CRM contacts and no data limits. Nutshell Marketing subscriptions require an additional monthly charge based on your number of marketable contacts.

So what’s a “marketing contact”?

Great question! A marketing contact is a person in Nutshell that has at least one email address and hasn’t unsubscribed from your communications or bounced from previous outreach.

Will I need IT to implement Nutshell?

Only if you spill your coffee on your keyboard. Nutshell is an out-of-the-box, cloud-based tool, so there’s no need to get the techies involved. And that means you can use it from anywhere, too.

Does Nutshell work on my mobile phone or tablet?

You bet it does. If you have an iPhone or Android phone, we offer apps that you can download for free in the App Store or Google Play Store.

If you use a tablet (like an iPad or Surface), Nutshell is responsively designed to work smoothly from your browser.

Does Nutshell work with my email?

Yep. Nutshell works with all email providers and clients, including Google and Outlook. You’ll never have to worry about your email not syncing with Nutshell.

What do I do if I need help?

Just contact us! We’ve got you covered. Prefer talking it out on the phone? Call us up at +1 (888) 336-8808 and you’ll be connected with our awesome support team in Ann Arbor, Michigan. We think that every single user—whether you’re just getting started with your trial or you’re a longtime customer—deserves phone support.

Why isn’t there an annual payment option for Nutshell Marketing?

It’s hard to predict how many marketing contacts a growing business will gain over the course of a year. Instead of asking our customers to predict how many contacts they think they’ll need during an annual subscription, we think it’s easier to charge Nutshell Marketing users month-to-month so you can increase your contact limit as needed.

I’m not paying for Nutshell Marketing, and yet I seem to have access to it in my Nutshell account. What’s going on here?

Don’t worry, we’re just giving you a little taste on the house. Nutshell customers can still send 150 delivered marketing emails per month (including test messages), even without a paid subscription.

With a paid Nutshell Marketing subscription, your team has a monthly send limit equal to 8X your contact limit.
